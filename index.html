Your small business management partner

At Blist, we are passionate about empowering small businesses to thrive in a competitive marketplace. We are a leading software company specializing in providing comprehensive Small Business Management Solutions and Services, encompassing accounting, finance, marketing, risk management, and analytics through our Bliven ERP system.

Our mission is to revolutionize the way small businesses operate by offering a suite of innovative software solutions that streamline operations, improve efficiency, and drive sustainable growth. From managing your finances and inventory to optimizing marketing campaigns and mitigating risks, our integrated tools empower you to make informed decisions and stay ahead of the curve.

We take pride in our customer-centric approach, tailoring our solutions to meet the specific needs of each business we work with. Our team of skilled professionals works closely with you, providing personalized support and guidance throughout your journey with us.
